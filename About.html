About us

Who are we and why?

We are a company established in November 2020. Our history as a team however can be tracked back to the early days of Counter-Strike: Source. Our goal as a company is to provide you with information about our webpage and the video game Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. We have multiple webpages with various different types of games, such as DotA 2, WoW, Fortnite, Rainbow Six Siege and Overwatch. We hope that you can enjoy our information and that you as a human take as much care as neccessary in these pandemic times. And with all the pleasure on our behalf.
